Lab 3: Serving fonts

font-style: Rubik Glitch Pop

The Rubik Glitch Pop font is a visually striking typeface that combines the clean lines of the original Rubik font with a unique glitch effect, creating a textured and modern appearance. Part of the Generative Fonts collection, this font was developed using script-generated techniques and is available under the SIL Open Font License, making it free for both personal and commercial use.

Its distinctive design makes it ideal for creative projects that require a contemporary and edgy look. You can find and download Rubik Glitch Pop from various font repositories, including Google Fonts and Fontesk.
